# Mail & Notification System

## Overview

The Mail & Notification System is the central communication hub of Roblox Buddy AI. It allows players to receive rewards, gifts, announcements, Buddy letters, achievements, trades, and friend interactions while providing complete notification customization and permanent mail history.

---

# 📬 Mail Types

## 👥 Friend Mail
- Friend Requests
- Best Friend Requests
- Friend Messages
- Friend Invitations

---

## 🎁 Gift Mail
- Gifts received from friends
- Reward claim mails
- Accept / Reject / Claim Later

---

## 🤝 Trade Mail
- Trade Requests
- Trade Completed
- Trade Cancelled
- Trade Expired

---

## 🏆 Achievement Mail
- Achievement unlocked
- Achievement rewards
- Secret achievement notifications

---

## 🎉 Event Mail
- Event Start
- Event Reminder
- Event Ending Soon
- Event Rewards

---

## 🤖 Buddy Mail

Buddy can personally send letters such as:

- Good Morning ❤️
- Good Night 🌙
- Birthday Wishes 🎂
- Congratulations 🎉
- Special Surprise Letters
- Funny Messages
- Motivation Messages

Buddy Mail cannot be disabled because it is part of Buddy's personality.

---

# Long-Term Goal

The Mail & Notification System will become the primary communication center of Roblox Buddy AI.
