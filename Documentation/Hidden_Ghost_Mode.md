# Hidden Ghost Mode Overview

Hidden Ghost Mode is a multiplayer horror mode where one player is secretly selected as the Ghost. The remaining players are Survivors who must complete objectives, discover the Ghost's identity, and perform a ritual to remove the Ghost from its host body.

## Players

Minimum Players: 3
Maximum Players: 8
1 Hidden Ghost
Remaining players are Survivors.

## Lobby System

### Private Lobby
Host creates the lobby.
Host can invite or remove players.
Only the Host can start the match.

### Public Lobby
Players can browse available rooms.
Join any public room.
Invite requests appear as notifications.
Story Mode remains Invite Only.

## Main Objective

### Survivors
Complete objectives.
Collect ritual items.
Identify the Hidden Ghost.
Perform the ritual.
Escape successfully.

### Ghost
Blend in with Survivors.
Pretend to complete tasks.
Mislead players.
Delay the ritual.
Eliminate the Survivors.

## Ghost Possession

The Ghost has a physical host body.

The Ghost can temporarily leave its own body and possess another player.

Possession Duration: 5–10 seconds.
After possession ends, the Ghost automatically returns to its original body.

During possession, the Ghost's original body remains frozen in place.
If Survivors discover the frozen body, they gain an important clue about the Ghost's identity.

## Ghost Energy System

The Ghost has:

100 Energy

Each power consumes Energy.

Example:
- Possession
- Door Lock
- Lights Off
- Fake Footsteps
- Teleport

Energy slowly regenerates over time.

This prevents the Ghost from becoming overpowered.

## Ghost Vision

The Ghost can:

See in darkness.
Detect Survivor aura.
Detect ritual energy.
Navigate the map more easily than Survivors.

## Fear System

Each Survivor has a Fear Meter.

Fear increases when:
- Ghost is nearby.
- A teammate dies.
- Strange sounds occur.
- Darkness increases.
- Jumpscares happen.

High Fear causes:
- Heartbeat sounds.
- Heavy breathing.
- Screen shake.
- Slight vision blur.

## Ritual System

The Ghost cannot be killed.

Instead, Survivors must find hidden ritual items.

Once every required item is collected, Survivors perform a ritual to force the Ghost out of its host body.

After the ritual:
- Ghost powers become weaker.
- Power cooldowns become longer.
- The Ghost becomes easier to defeat.

## Random Systems

Every match changes automatically.

Randomized:
- Ritual item locations.
- Puzzle locations.
- Secret rooms.
- Escape routes.

No two matches are exactly the same.

## Hidden Achievements

Players can unlock secret achievements through special gameplay actions.

Examples:
- Perfect Escape
- Silent Survivor
- Ghost Hunter
- Never Seen
- Master Detective

These achievements remain hidden until unlocked.
