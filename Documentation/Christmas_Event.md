# 🎄 Christmas Event

## Overview

Christmas is one of the biggest global events in Roblox Buddy AI.

Theme:

🎄 Fun

🎁 Gifts

🍪 Cookies

🤖 Buddy

🎅 Santa

👥 Multiplayer

Unlike Halloween, Christmas focuses on joy, teamwork and celebration.

15 Days

# 👹 Frozen Giant Showdown

Players:

2–10

One random player becomes:

👹 Giant Frozen Yeti

Everyone else becomes:

😊 Survivors

---

## Giant Frozen Yeti

Health:

1000 HP

Goal:

Stop everyone from reaching the Cannons.

The Yeti does not directly fight players.

Instead it creates obstacles.

---

## Survivor Objective

Players must:

- Complete Frozen Parkour
- Reach Cannon Area
- Enter Cannon
- Shoot Giant Frozen Yeti

Each Cannon Shot:

25 Damage

Total:

40 Successful Hits

---

# 🔄 Respawn

Players falling into the ice:

Respawn after:

2 Seconds

Respawn at Start Area.

Must complete parkour again.

---

# ❄️ Giant Frozen Yeti Shop

The Giant Frozen Yeti earns Ice Tokens during the match.

It purchases:

- Ice Walls
- Slippery Floors
- Snow Wind
- Freeze Zones
- Ice Spikes
- Snow Storm
- Ice Bridges
- Moving Obstacles

These are placed across the parkour.

---

# ⚡ Giant Frozen Yeti Special Powers

Special Powers cost extra Ice Tokens.

Examples:

- Freeze Players (2 Seconds)
- Entire Floor Slippery
- Blizzard
- Giant Ice Wall
- Snow Tornado
- Ice Trap

---

# 😊 Survivor Shop

Players earn event currency during the match.

Temporary upgrades:

- Helmet
- Speed Boots
- Double Jump
- Shield
- Better Cannon Aim
- Faster Reload

All upgrades reset after every match.

# ❤️ Inspiration

Frozen Giant Showdown was inspired by an old multiplayer game remembered by the project creator.

Although the original game is no longer available, its joyful multiplayer feeling lives on through this original Christmas mini-game created for Roblox Buddy AI.
